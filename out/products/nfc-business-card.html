TapConnect NFC

NFC-powered digital business cards

A modern NFC-powered digital business card platform that enables instant contact sharing through a simple tap or QR code scan. Replace paper cards with a customisable digital profile that updates in real-time, tracks engagement analytics, and integrates with your CRM.

Request a Demo

✦ Key Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Tap-to-Share

Share your full contact profile instantly by tapping your NFC card against any smartphone — no app required for the recipient.

Custom Digital Profiles

Fully branded profile pages with your photo, bio, social links, portfolio, and any custom fields your business needs.

Real-Time Updates

Change your job title, phone number, or links anytime — every card and QR code automatically reflects the latest info.

Engagement Analytics

Track who viewed your profile, which links they clicked, and when — with exportable reports and CRM sync.

QR Code Fallback

Every profile includes a unique QR code for situations where NFC is not available — works on any device with a camera.

Team Management

Admin dashboard to provision, manage, and brand cards for entire teams with role-based access and bulk operations.
